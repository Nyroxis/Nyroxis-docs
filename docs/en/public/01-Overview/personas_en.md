# Target Personas

Nyroxis is designed for real people who need stronger protection than traditional consumer security tools can provide.  
Below are the primary personas the platform is built for.

---

## Executives & High-Profile Individuals
Executives and public figures are frequent targets of:
- Credential theft  
- Social engineering  
- Targeted malware  
- Surveillance attempts  

Nyroxis gives them local visibility into suspicious activity without relying on external cloud services.

---

## Lawyers, Doctors & Sensitive Professions
Professionals handling confidential data need:
- Strong privacy  
- tamper-resistant event logging  
- early detection of anomalies  

Nyroxis helps protect devices that store legal documents, medical files, contracts, and confidential communications.

---

## Families & Everyday Users
Families often lack tools that explain:
- What is happening on their computer  
- Whether something suspicious is occurring  
- What risks exist for children and teenagers  

Nyroxis provides simple visibility and peace of mind.

---

## Cybersecurity Enthusiasts & SOC Analysts at Home
Experts want:
- A personal SIEM-style timeline  
- Behavioral insights  
- Local encrypted logging  
- No cloud dependencies  

Nyroxis gives them a structured environment for monitoring their own devices.

---

## Remote Workers & Frequent Travelers
Travelers and remote professionals face:
- Untrusted networks  
- Increased exposure  
- High risk of credential theft  

Nyroxis offers a portable, reliable, offline-first security layer that travels with the user.

---

## Summary
Nyroxis is built for anyone who needs clarity and protection at the personal level — from families to high‑profile professionals, and from remote workers to cybersecurity practitioners.
