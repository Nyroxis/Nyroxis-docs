# FAQ

## ❓ Is Nyroxis fully offline?
Yes. No cloud, no telemetry, no data transmission.

## ❓ Does Nyroxis read personal files?
No. It only analyzes technical security events.

## ❓ Can logs be exported?
Only encrypted backups (future feature).

## ❓ Is AI local?
100% local — no cloud inference.

## ❓ Who is Nyroxis for?
Families, executives, freelancers, and technical users.
